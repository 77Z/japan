Hello Japan!

By: Lauren Edwards

DAY ONE

When I was coming on the plane I think a main question is what are great tourist attractions in japan? We arive at the Kiyomizu-dera which holds a very stange tradition, in Japan Kannon the god of mercy is a beleif in Japan so people go to this shrine, before the law was changed in 1864 people would do this thing called "taking the plunge" which is a very advanced form of wish making pretty much you jump from the 13 meter building, and don't die. If your put life of the hands of kannon and jump, you will be granted a wish! Unless you don't die (Because kannon doesn't give wishes to corpses) According to the manuscript, 234 people jumped from the stage of Kiyomizu. A woman tried to jump even twice. Survival rate was about 85% (34 people died). After visting the shrine we go to the Shiono (塩野) in Akasaka which serves Mochi.Mochi is Japanese rice cake made of mochigome, a short-grain japonica glutinous rice, and sometimes other ingredients such as water, sugar, and cornstarch. The rice is pounded into paste and molded into the desired shape. Then we head down the streets of Dōtonbori a poular street know for it's food, shops and theater! Stop at a restaurant and crash into bed.

DAY TWO

Waking up knowing today is a day to see all the beauty in Japan! The first part of today is to grab a quick breakfest at Kanetanaka So 金田中 草 [Shibuya] then heading to Chūbu region, Honshu, Japan the national park that has Mount. Fuji, a truly beautiful sight it's last Last eruption was on December 16, 1707 for now it's been dormant. Heading over to the studio ghibli museum pretty much if you love any form of art you will love this place. Then in the museum there is a cafe called The Staw Hat Cafe which serves really good food that is also seasonal so the menu depends on what season you come in. Over to the Narusawa Hyoketsu which is an ice cave, 70 foot lava cave with blue glowing ice crystal. While you walk you will see crystal up to 3 feet long! After we go to the very famous from the Japenese anime called "Naruto" the ramen restaurant called Ichiraku Ramen Shime Shiten and eat some of Naruto Shippūden's favorite ramen, Miso Chashu Pork bowl. Then go to sleep thinking of the next day.
